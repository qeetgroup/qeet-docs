## What is Qeet People?

Qeet People is the **human capital management (HCM)** product in the Qeet suite. It models
your organization — people, teams, roles, and reporting lines — and shares one identity
graph with [Qeet ID](/id), so a hire in People is a user in ID without a sync job.
