Qeet People is planned as a phased build from core HR toward a full human-capital management
suite, India-first throughout.

## Phase 1 — MVP (months 0–6)

Core HR that can safely run day-to-day operations: **core HR & employee management,
attendance & leave, basic India payroll, employee and manager self-service, basic reporting,
and India statutory compliance** (PF, ESI, PT, TDS) — plus a basic mobile app, WhatsApp
self-service for leave and payslips, and explainable-AI governance from the start.

## Phase 2 — Growth (months 6–12)

The complete HR suite for mid-market: **recruitment & ATS, onboarding/offboarding,
performance management, learning, compensation, advanced payroll** (EWA, full-and-final,
full compliance), analytics, a basic AI copilot, and regional-language UI (Hindi, Tamil,
Telugu).

## Phase 3 — Differentiation (months 12–18)

Capabilities that set the product apart: **financial wellness, mental health & wellbeing,
agentic AI automation, skills & talent intelligence, workforce planning**, broader regional
language coverage, and a frontline/blue-collar module.

## Phase 4 — Market leadership (months 18–24)

Enterprise and global depth: **global compliance, expat/assignment management, ESG/BRSR
reporting, a board & C-suite dashboard, and lifecycle prediction** — with expansion beyond
India.
