## What is Qeet Pay?

Qeet Pay is the **payments, billing, and financial infrastructure** product in the Qeet suite.
It brings everything a modern business needs to move money — collect payments, run
subscriptions, generate GST-compliant invoices, disburse payouts, and settle marketplaces —
into **one API, one dashboard, and one integration**.

It's built **India-first**: UPI, NACH, cards, net banking, and wallets alongside GST
e-invoicing and compliance — with multi-tenancy and the [Qeet ecosystem](/id) treated as
first-class concerns from day one.

## Where it fits in the platform

Every Qeet product needs to bill and disburse — [Qeet People](/people) pays salaries and
collects HR-SaaS fees, [Qeet Logs](/logs) bills usage-based ingest, and [Qeet ID](/id) bills
per active user. Rather than each integrating a separate payment gateway, billing tool, and GST
filer, Qeet Pay is the shared financial layer they all run on, with [Qeet ID](/id) for
subscriber identity and Qeet Logs for the financial audit trail.

## Want early access?

Pay is being designed alongside the rest of the platform. Until it ships, [Qeet ID](/id) is
live today and provides the identity and tenant model the billing layer will build on.
