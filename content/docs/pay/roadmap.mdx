## Phase 1 — Foundation

_Targeting H2 2026._ The goal is the first external merchants live, with every Qeet Group
product billing and disbursing through Qeet Pay.

- **Acceptance:** UPI (collect, intent, QR), cards (3DS 2.0 + RBI tokenization), net banking,
  wallets, payment links, hosted checkout, and a JS + React SDK.
- **Payouts:** UPI / IMPS / NEFT, single and bulk, with maker-checker approval and refunds.
- **Billing:** subscription lifecycle and proration, flat / per-unit / tiered pricing, NACH &
  UPI AutoPay mandates, and rule-based dunning.
- **GST invoicing:** CGST/SGST/IGST auto-calculation, HSN/SAC lookup, credit & debit notes,
  multi-currency invoices, and a GSTR-1 data export.
- **Developer experience:** REST API v1, Go + TypeScript + Python + Java SDKs, an OpenAPI spec,
  a sandbox with test rails, and a `qp` CLI.
- **Compliance:** KYB onboarding, India data residency, and rule-based fraud scoring.

## Phase 2 — Depth & intelligence

_Targeting H1 2027._ E-invoicing, GST auto-filing, and AI throughout.

- **Acceptance:** UPI 2.0 and AutoPay mandate management, e-NACH, virtual accounts, Bharat QR,
  and mobile SDKs (Flutter, React Native, Android, iOS).
- **AI features:** AI dunning to recover failed AutoPay, ML payment orchestration, an ML fraud
  model, cash-flow forecasting, and HSN/SAC classification.
- **GST & compliance:** IRN e-invoicing via the IRP, GSTR-1 auto-filing, TDS/TCS tracking, and
  multi-GSTIN management.
- **Billing:** usage-based and hybrid pricing, an MRR waterfall, and revenue recognition
  (IndAS 115 / IFRS 15).
- **WhatsApp & marketplace:** WhatsApp invoice delivery (combined e-invoice + UPI QR) and
  marketplace split payments with per-seller GST attribution.

## Phase 3 — Enterprise & global

_Targeting H2 2027._ Embedded finance, cross-border payments, and enterprise treasury features
as licensing and scale allow.
