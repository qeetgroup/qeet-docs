## What is Qeet Logs?

Qeet Logs is a **log management platform** for teams that take privacy seriously. It's
multi-tenant and **identity-aware** — every line is attributable through [Qeet ID](/id) —
so you can answer "who did what" without bolting on a separate audit pipeline.
