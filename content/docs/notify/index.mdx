## What is Qeet Notify?

Qeet Notify is the **multi-channel notification platform** for the Qeet suite. One unified API
orchestrates messages across **email, SMS, WhatsApp, mobile & web push, and in-app** — with
multi-tenancy, India-first compliance, and [Qeet ID](/id) subscriber identity treated as
architecture decisions, not afterthoughts.

It's built for engineering teams at multi-tenant SaaS companies who today stitch together
separate vendors for each channel — and rebuild preference centers, suppression logic, and
compliance plumbing for every product they launch.

## Where it fits in the platform

Every Qeet product needs notifications — Qeet ID sends OTPs, magic links, and security alerts;
[Qeet People](/people) sends payroll and approval messages; [Qeet Logs](/logs) sends alert
notifications. Rather than each product integrating email, SMS, WhatsApp, and push vendors
independently, Qeet Notify is the shared layer they all send through.

## Want early access?

Notify is being designed alongside the rest of the platform. Until it ships, [Qeet ID](/id)
already sends the identity-related messages — sign-in, MFA, and webhooks — that Notify will
eventually carry.
